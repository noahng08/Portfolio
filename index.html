About Me

Growing up in a creative family, I've always thrived on unstructured thinking and novel ideas. School's focus on memorizing formulas for good grades left me dissatisfied, so I sought opportunities to explore my creativity. In 6th grade, I won a Bronze medal in the Vietnamese National Creative Contest for Youth with a simple yet innovative miniature of my middle school‚Äîno formulas, just imagination.

Two years ago, I joined the University of Waterloo, drawn by its networking pool despite missing the co-op program. Early on, I connected with a fellow student who introduced me to Data Science, a field that felt like a perfect fit for my curiosity. Over time, I discovered a passion for generating insights and found my place in the Product track, where creativity meets analytical thinking.

I've had experience across industries like EdTech, Finance, and Insurance, applying statistical methods and AI to solve business challenges. I'm now looking for opportunities where my unique perspective and skills can make an impact.

Outside work, I'm building a big project and find inspiration while solo traveling, often using these moments to brainstorm new ideas. I'm also a music enthusiast, studying piano professionally, keeping active through soccer, and contributing volunteering work pro bono to non-profit causes. My goals are to have a fulfilling life by following my passions and making a difference in my own way.
